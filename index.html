01. Legal Preamble and Identification

This Comprehensive Legal Framework ("Agreement") establishes the terms of relationship between RizqDaan Platform (hereinafter "the Company", "We", "Us", or "Our") and any person or entity accessing the RizqDaan Mobile Application (hereinafter "the User", "Vendor", or "You").

RizqDaan operates as a digital intermediary platform specializing in multi-vendor business listings, service provision advertising, and peer-to-peer commerce specifically optimized for the Pakistani demographic. By creating an account, posting a listing, or browsing content, you explicitly and irrevocably consent to the clauses outlined in this document. If you find any provision herein objectionable, you are mandated to terminate your session and uninstall the software immediately.

Constitutional Binding

This agreement satisfies the requirements of the Electronic Transactions Ordinance, 2002, and the Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA), 2016, of Pakistan. It serves as a binding electronic contract with the same legal standing as a physical deed.

02. Data Acquisition and Classification

Our infrastructure is designed to maintain a high-trust marketplace environment. Consequently, we collect three primary tiers of data:

A. Identity and KYC Data

Upon registration, we require: Full Legal Name, Verified Mobile Number (linked to CNIC via external verification where applicable), Electronic Mail Address, and Precise Business Location. For Vendors, we additionally store Shop Name, Business Address, and verification status.

B. Behavioral and Content Metadata

This includes every listing created, image uploaded, chat interaction initiated within the application, search history, items marked as favorites, and interactions with promoted/featured content. We store these to ensure platform safety and provide personalized recommendations.

C. Technical Device Forensics

To prevent multi-account fraud and bot activities, we collect device-specific identifiers including IMEI, MAC Address, IP Address (for geo-locking), OS version, and battery health telemetry to distinguish human users from automated scripts.

04. Disclosure to Third-Party Nodes

RizqDaan leverages international-grade cloud infrastructure. Your data is processed by:

  • Google Firebase Infrastructure: For real-time database management, secure authentication tokens, and push notification delivery. All data resides in secure regional clusters.
  • Cloudinary Global CDN: All visual media (photos/videos) are stored and optimized via Cloudinary's encrypted storage facilities to ensure rapid loading across Pakistan.
  • Gemini AI (Google LLM): If you utilize the "AI Description Writer," your keywords are sent to Google's LLM models to generate professional ad text. No PII is attached to these prompts.
  • Local Payment Gateway Partners: Transaction IDs for wallet top-ups are verified via secure APIs. We never store your EasyPaisa/JazzCash PINs or passwords.

05. Data Sovereignty and Account Deletion

We respect your "Right to be Forgotten." In full compliance with Google Play Store's Data Safety policies, we provide a transparent deletion protocol.

Deletion Execution Steps:

  1. Access the Account interface in the bottom navigation.
  2. Engage the Settings (Gear Icon).
  3. Locate and trigger "Delete Account Permanently".
  4. Verify identity via PIN or Password.

System Impact: Upon confirmation, your profile is immediately hidden from public view. Our automated scrubbers will purge your listings, chats, and personal identifiers from our live servers within 48 hours. Legal backups (transactional data for tax auditing) are retained for 7 years in an anonymized, non-reversible format as per Pakistan law.
